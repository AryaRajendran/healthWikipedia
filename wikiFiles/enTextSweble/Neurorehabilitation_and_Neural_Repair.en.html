Neurorehabilitation and Neural Repair is a peer-reviewed medical journal that publishes papers in the fields of rehabilitation and clinical neurology. The editor-in-chief is Bruce H. Dobkin (Geffen School of Medicine). It was established in 1987 and is currently published by SAGE Publications in association with American Society of Neurorehabilitation. Neurorehabilitation and Neural Repair is abstracted and indexed in Scopus and the Social Sciences Citation Index. According to the Journal Citation Reports, its 2010 impact factor is 3.772, ranking it 1 out of 44 journals in the category "Rehabilitation" and 37 out of 185 journals in the category "Clinical Neurology".
