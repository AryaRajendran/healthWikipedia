 Current Medical Research and Opinion is a peer-reviewed medical journal established in 1972. It is published monthly by Informa. The journal is abstracted and indexed by MEDLINE/PubMed, EMBASE/Excerpta Medica, Current Contents/Clinical Medicine, Science Citation Index, CAB Abstracts, PASCAL, and PsycINFO. According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal's 2009 impact factor is 2.498.
