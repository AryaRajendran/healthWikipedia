 The Journal of Oncology Pharmacy Practice is a peer-reviewed academic journal that publishes papers four times a year in the field of Pharmacology. The journal's editor is Barry R. Goldspiel. It has been in publication since 1995 and is currently published by SAGE Publications in association with the International Society of Oncology and Pharmacy Practitioners. The Journal of Oncology Pharmacy Practice is dedicated to educating health professionals about providing pharmaceutical care to patients with cancer. The journal publishes case reports and consensus guidelines, information on new products and research reviews. The Journal of Oncology Pharmacy Practice also contains regular updates on ISOPP activities, as well as practical issues relating to oncology pharmacy and worker safety.
