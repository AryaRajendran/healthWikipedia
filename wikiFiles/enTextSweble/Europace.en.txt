 Europace (European Journal of Pacing, Arrhythmias and Cardiac Electrophysiology) is a peer-reviewed medical journal that publishes research articles about the study and management of cardiac dysrhythmia, cardiac pacing, and cardiac cellular electrophysiology. Europace is the official journal of the European Society of Cardiology and the European Heart Rhythm Association.
