 Normocytic anemia is a common issue that occurs for men and women typically over 85 years old. Its prevalence increases with age, reaching 44 percent in men older than 85 years. Normocytic anemia is the most frequently encountered type of anemia. A normocytic anemia is defined as an anemia with an MCV of 80-100 which is the normal range. However, the hematocrit and hemoglobin is decreased.
