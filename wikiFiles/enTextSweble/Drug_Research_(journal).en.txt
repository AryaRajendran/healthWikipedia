 Drug Research is a peer-reviewed medical journal covering drug development published by Thieme Medical Publishers. The journal was established in 1951 and the editor-in-chief is Martin Wehling (Heidelberg University Faculty of Medicine in Mannheim). Drug Research is abstracted an indexed in: According to the Journal Citation Reports, it has a 2011 impact factor of 0.722, ranking it 56th out of 59 journals in the category "Chemistry, multidisciplinary" and 222nd out of 261 journals in the category "Pharmacology & Pharmacy".
