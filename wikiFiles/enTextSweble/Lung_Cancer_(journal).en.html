Lung Cancer is the official peer-reviewed medical journal published by Elsevier on behalf of the International Association for the Study of Lung Cancer. It includes original research and review articles of relevance to lung cancer. The journal is abstracted and indexed in Current Contents/Clinical Medicine, EMBASE, BIOBASE, MEDLINE, Oncology Information Service, SciSearch, and Scopus. According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal's 2009 impact factor is 3.140, ranking it 62nd out of 166 journals in the category "Oncology" and 10th out of 43 in the category "Respiratory System".
