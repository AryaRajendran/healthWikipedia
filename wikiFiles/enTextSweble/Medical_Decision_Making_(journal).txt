 Medical Decision Making is a peer-reviewed academic journal that publishes papers in the fields of decision making and medical informatics. Its editor-in-chief is Mark Helfand (Portland VA Medical Center). It was established in 1981 and is currently published by SAGE Publications on behalf of the Society for Medical Decision Making. Medical Decision Making is abstracted and indexed in Scopus and the Social Sciences Citation Index. According to the Journal Citation Reports, its 2011 impact factor is 2.329, ranking it 5th out of 23 journals in the category "Medical Informatics" and 21st out of 76 journals in the category "Health Care Sciences & Services".
