 The Journal of Asthma & Allergy Educators is a bimonthly peer-reviewed medical journal that covers research in the fields of pulmonary disorders and allergy, especially asthma, allergy management, and patient education. The editor-in-chief is Concettina Tolomeo (Yale School of Medicine). The journal was established in 2010 and is currently published by SAGE Publications in association with the Association of Asthma Educators. The Journal of Asthma & Allergy Educators is abstracted and indexed in Scopus.
