 Malnutrition is when a person does not get the nutrition they need to live. It is considered a more scientific and official term than starvation which has negative associations. Malnutrition is not, however, simply not eating enough. Someone can be malnourished if they eat the wrong balance of foods. Malnutrition is one of the biggest problems in India because most of the people do not get the right amount of food to eat, hence increasing the death rate.
