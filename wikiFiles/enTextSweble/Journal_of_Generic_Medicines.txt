 The Journal of Generic Medicines is a peer-reviewed academic journal that publishes papers four times a year in the field of Management. The journal's editor is M. Fabiana Jorge (MFJ International). It has been in publication since 2001 and is currently published by SAGE Publications. The Journal of Generic Medicines is written by and for professionals working in the generic medicines sector. The journal includes expert analysis, briefings and updates on all aspects of business developments, regulatory affairs and marketing that may have an effect of the generic pharmaceutical industry.
