 Asian Cardiovascular and Thoracic Annals is a bimonthly peer-reviewed academic journal that covers research in the fields of cardiology and cardiovascular medicine. The editor-in-chief is A. Sampath Kumar (Pushpanjali Crosslay Hospital). It was established in 1998 and is currently published by SAGE Publications in association with the Asian Society for Cardiovascular and Thoracic Surgery.
