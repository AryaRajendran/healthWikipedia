 The American Journal of Medicine is a peer-reviewed medical journal and the official journal of the Association of Professors of Medicine. It publishes original clinical research in internal medicine. It is published monthly by Elsevier under its Excerpta Medica imprint. The journal is abstracted and indexed in BIOSIS, CINAHL, Chemical Abstracts, Current Contents, EMBASE, Global Health, MEDLINE, Science Citation Index, and Scopus.
