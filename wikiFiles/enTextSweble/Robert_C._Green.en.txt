 Robert C. Green is a researcher in clinical genetics at the Division of Genetics and Department of Medicine at Brigham and Women's Hospital and Harvard Medical School working in translational genomics and the integration of personal genomics into clinical practice. His research includes the return of incidental findings in clinical settings, and topics in Alzheimer's disease, including the development of risk estimates based on family history and genetic markers and the return of these risk factors to patients.
