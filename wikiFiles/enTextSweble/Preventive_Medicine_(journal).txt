 Preventive Medicine is a peer-reviewed medical journal published by Elsevier since 1972. It covers all aspects of preventive medicine and public health. The editor-in-chief is Alfredo Morabia (City University of New York). The founding editor was Ernst Wynder. The journal is abstracted and indexed in EMBASE, Scopus, Science Citation Index, Current Contents/Clinical Medicine, BIOSIS Previews, and Index Medicus/MEDLINE/PubMed. Its 2010 impact factor was 3.299, ranking it 20th among 151 journals in the category "General and Internal Medicine" and 20th among 140 journals in the category "Public, Environmental, and Occupational Health".
