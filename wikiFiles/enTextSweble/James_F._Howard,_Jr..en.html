James Francis Howard, Jr. (born May 3 1948) is a Professor of Neurology and Medicine at University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Howard was born 1948-05-03 in Bellows Falls, Vermont, U.S.. He received a BA in 1970 and a M.D. in 1974, both from the University of Vermont. Howard became an Assistant Professor of Neurology and Medicine at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill in 1979, was promoted to Associate Professor in 1985, and promoted to full Professor in 1992. He is also an Adjunct Professor of Clinical Sciences (Neurology) at the North Carolina State University.
