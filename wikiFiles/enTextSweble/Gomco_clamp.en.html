A Gomco clamp, otherwise known as a Yellen clamp, is a specialized clamp for performing circumcisions. It was invented by Hiram S. Yellen and Aaron Goldstein in 1934-1935. Gomco stands for the GOldstein Medical COmpany, the original manufacturer of the clamp. In a 1998 study, it was the preferred circumcision technique of 67% of US physicians.
