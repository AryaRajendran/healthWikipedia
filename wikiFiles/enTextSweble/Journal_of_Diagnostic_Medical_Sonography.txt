 The Journal of Diagnostic Medical Sonography is a bimonthly peer-reviewed medical journal that covers research in the field of diagnostic imaging. Its editor-in-chief is Phillip Bendick. It has been in publication since 1985 and is currently published by SAGE Publications in association with the Society of Diagnostic Medical Sonography. The Journal of Diagnostic Medical Sonography is abstracted and indexed in Academic Onefile, EMBASE, and Scopus.
