 The deep perineal pouch (also deep perineal space) is a human anatomical term that refers to the space enclosed in part by the perineum, and located superior to the perineal membrane. The deep perineal pouch is bordered inferiorly by the perineal membrane, also known as the inferior fascia of the urogenital diaphragm. It is bordered superiorly by the superior fascia of the urogenital diaphragm. The deep pouch is now described as the region between the perineal membrane and pelvic diaphragm.
