 Foot & Ankle Specialist is a bimonthly peer-reviewed medical journal that coversn the field of orthopedics. The editors-in-chief are Gregory C. Berlet and Lowell Weil, Jr. (Weil Foot & Ankle Institute). It was established 2008 and is currently published by SAGE Publications. Foot & Ankle Specialist is abstracted and indexed in PubMed, Medline, EMBASE, and Scopus.
