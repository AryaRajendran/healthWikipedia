 The Curie Institute or Institut Curie is one of the leading medical research centres in the world. It is a private non-profit foundation for research into cancer. It is located in Paris, France. It was founded by Marie Curie and got state approval as a foundation in 1921. There are several hundred research staff at the Institute.Institut Curie does not offer undergrduate degrees, but does award Ph.D.s. Institut Curie runs the Hôpital Claudius Régaud, a cancer treatment hospital. Five Nobel prizes are attached to the Institute's researchers.
