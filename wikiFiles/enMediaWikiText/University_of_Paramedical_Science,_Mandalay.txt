 The University of Paramedical Science, Mandalay (, ), located in Patheingyi, Mandalay, is one of three institutions of higher learning specialized in paramedical science in Myanmar. The university offers four-year Bachelor of Paramedical Science (BPSc) degree programs for Physiotherapy, Radiography and Medical Technology. The university accepts 180 students a year. The university offers the following three four-year BPSc programs. Each program accepts 60 students a year. The language of instruction is English. The university consists of 12 academic departments.
