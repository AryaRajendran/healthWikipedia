 The Texas Academy of Family Physicians (TAFP) is a medical specialty organization headquartered in Austin, Texas, USA. The academy is governed by a board of directors, composed of the TAFP Leadership and TAFP members from various local chapters. The board establishes and reviews policy and plans of the academy during the TAFP Interim Session and Annual Session meetings. Members also serve on TAFP committees, commissions, task forces and sections where they study, analyze and make policy recommendations to the board.
