 The Pan-African Society of Hypertension(PASH,Inc.) is an international organization with the objective to provide a stable and organized African platform for scientific exchange in arterial hypertension. The society was created in 2010 on the initiative of Marc Twagirumukiza. The official membership is obtained after registration.
