 The World Journal for Pediatric and Congenital Heart Surgery is a peer-reviewed academic journal that publishes papers four times a year in the field of Cardiovascular Disease. The journal's editor is Marshall Jacobs, MD (Center for Pediatric and Congenital Heart Diseases, Cleveland Clinic). It has been in publication since 2010 and is currently published by SAGE Publications in association with World Society for Pediatric and Congenital Heart Surgery. The World Journal for Pediatric and Congenital Heart Surgery is dedicated to the advancement and dissemination of knowledge relating to congenital heart anomalies and pediatric heart diseases in general. The journal focuses on the surgical aspect of the care of infants, children, and adults.
