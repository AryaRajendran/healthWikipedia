 Proteins : Structure, Function, and Bioinformatics is a monthly peer-reviewed scientific journal published by [[John Wiley & Sons]], which was established in 1986 by Cyrus Levinthal. The journal covers research on all aspects protein biochemistry, including computation, function, structure, design, and genetics. The editor-in-chief is Bertrand Garcia-Moreno (Johns Hopkins University). Publishing formats are original research reports, short communications, prediction reports, invited reviews, and topic proposals. In addition, Proteins includes a section entitled "Section Notes", describing novel protein structures.
