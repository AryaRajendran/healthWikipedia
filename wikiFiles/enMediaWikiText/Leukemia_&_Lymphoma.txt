 Leukemia & Lymphoma is a peer-reviewed medical journal published by [[Taylor & Francis|Taylor and Francis]]. It includes full-length papers and reviews on basic and clinical aspects of hematologic malignancies. The editors-in-chief of Leukemia & Lymphoma are Aaron Polliack, Koen Van Besien, and John Saymour. The journal is abstracted and indexed in the Web of Science, PubMed, Chemical Abstracts, and others. According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2009 impact factor of 2.397.
