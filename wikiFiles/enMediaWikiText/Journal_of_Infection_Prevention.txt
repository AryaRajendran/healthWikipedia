 The Journal of Infection Prevention is a peer-reviewed academic journal that publishes papers six times a year in the field of [[Health & Nursing]]. The journal's editor is Heather Loveday (University of West London). It has been in publication since 2000 and is currently published by SAGE Publications on behalf of Infection Prevention Society. The Journal of Infection Prevention aims to advance evidence and research in the field of infection prevention and control. The journal publishes original research, qualitative and quantitative research studies, case studies and overviews of infectious diseases and their causes. The Journal of Infection Prevention provides a forum for health professionals interested in this field or practice.
