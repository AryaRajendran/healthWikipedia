 SAMPLE history is an mnemonic acronym to remember key questions for a person's assessment. The SAMPLE history is usually taken along with vital signs. The questions are most commonly used in the field of emergency medicine by police officers and EMTs. It is used for alert people, but often much of this information can also be obtained from the family or friend of an unresponsive person.
