 Qualitative Health Research is a peer-reviewed academic journal that publishes papers in the field of Health. The journal's editor is Janice M. Morse, RN, PhD(Nurs), PhD (Anthro), FAAN (University of Utah). It has been in publication since 1991 and is currently published by SAGE Publications. Qualitative Health Research aims to provide a resource for researchers and academics, administrators and others in the health and social service professions as well as graduates who seek examples of qualitative methods. The journal is an interdisciplinary forum which focuses on health care and the further development and understanding of qualitative research in health-care settings. Qualitative Health Research is abstracted and indexed in, among other databases: SCOPUS, and the Social Sciences Citation Index. According to the Journal Citation Reports, its 2010 impact factor is 2.264, ranking it 13 out of 58 journals in the category ‘Health Policy & Services’.
