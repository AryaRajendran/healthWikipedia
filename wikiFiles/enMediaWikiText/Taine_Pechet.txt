 Taine Pechet is a thoracic surgeon and Vice Chief of Surgery at the University of Pennsylvania's Presbyterian Medical Center. Pechet has made substantial contributions to the field of thoracic surgery. He resides in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania. Pechet was recognized in Philadelphia Magazine's 2011 Top Docs issue. Pechet specializes in lung cancer, lung surgery, video assisted thoracic surgery (vats), palliative care.
