 Anesthesia & Analgesia is a monthly peer-reviewed medical journal covering anesthesia, pain management, and perioperative medicine that was established in 1922. It is published by [[Lippincott Williams & Wilkins]] on behalf of the International Anesthesia Research Society. Its editor-in-chief is Steven L. Shafer (Columbia University). According to the Journal Citation Reports, the journal has a 2010 impact factor of 3.274. [[Category:Lippincott Williams & Wilkins academic journals]]
