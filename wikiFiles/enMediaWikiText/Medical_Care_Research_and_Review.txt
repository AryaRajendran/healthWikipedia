 Medical Care Research and Review is a peer-reviewed academic journal that covers the field of health care. The editor-in-chief is Gloria J. Bazzoli (Virginia Commonwealth University). It was established in 1944 and is currently published by SAGE Publications. The journal publishes three types of articles: review articles on particular research or policy topics that synthesize theoretical and empirical literature across several disciplines; empirical research; and articles that present new data and trends in the health care field.
