 The Journal of Transcultural Nursing is a peer-reviewed academic journal that publishes papers in the field of Surgery. The journal's editors are Adrian E. Park, MD, FRCS (Dalhousie University) and Lee Swanstrom, MD (Legacy Health System). It has been in publication since 1994 and is currently published by SAGE Publications. The Journal of Transcultural Nursing aims to offer nurses, educators, researchers and practitioners theoretical approaches and current research findings that have direct implications for the delivery of culturally congruent health care and for the preparation of health care professionals who will provide that care. The journal provides a forum for the discussion of topics that affect nursing and health care clinical practice, research and theory development. The Journal of Transcultural Nursing is abstracted and indexed in, among other databases: SCOPUS, and the Social Sciences Citation Index. According to the Journal Citation Reports, its 2010 impact factor is 0.714, ranking it 63 out of 89 journals in the category ‘Nursing’. and 60 out of 87 journals in the category ‘Nursing’.
